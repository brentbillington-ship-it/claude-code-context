# Family Office Tracker — Aesthetics & UI Agent

> **Reviews UI/aesthetic patterns from Monarch Money (primary reference), Vyzer, Kubera, and best-in-class fintech/SaaS dashboards. Identifies which patterns to adopt for the v1 family office tracker UI.**

## When to Invoke

- v1 design phase needs grounded UI direction
- Refresh on competitor UI as benchmarks evolve

## Prerequisites

- Public marketing pages, review screenshots, YouTube product walkthroughs accessible
- No login attempts; no scraping gated content

## Playbook

1. Deep-dive Monarch Money — primary reference for user-friendliness. Document navigation, dashboard composition, transaction list design, account list, color, typography, empty states, mobile responsive behavior.
2. Deep-dive Vyzer (Premium + Family Office tiers) and Kubera (Essentials + Black).
3. Survey family-office/wealth tools: Asora, Addepar (public materials only), Eton AtlasFive, Capitally.
4. Survey adjacent fintech with strong UI: Copilot Money, Lunch Money, Empower, Stripe Dashboard, Carta investor portal.
5. Survey general "best UI" benchmarks: Linear, Notion, Vercel dashboard, Cron / Notion Calendar.
6. Synthesis section identifies which patterns to adopt for the family office tracker.

## Success Criteria

- Per-tool sections covering navigation, layout, typography, color, density, empty/loading states
- Monarch gets dedicated extended section
- Synthesis with prioritized adoption list
- ≥5 distinct cited sources
- No image downloads, URLs only

## Failure Modes & Recovery

- **Login wall blocks deep-dive on a tool:** Document what's visible from public marketing, mark internal screens as inferred from reviews.
- **Tool's UI has changed since last review article:** Note publication date, weight recent walkthroughs higher.
