# Family Office Tracker — Pricing & Positioning Agent

> **Produces a defensible pricing recommendation for a family-office tracker engagement, grounded in published competitor pricing, freelance/agency rates, and a decision tree across JJ-anchor scenarios.**

## When to Invoke

- Discovery complete, written proposal due
- Pricing recalibration needed mid-engagement
- New family-office prospect requires comparable benchmarking

## Prerequisites

- Discovery recap available with: client volume signals, hosting preference, scope boundaries, any anchor numbers Brent has heard
- BB-Notes pricing posture (portfolio rate + referral commission, never lead with free, ~60-80hr deep-discount walk-away)
- Brent's stated context (first paid BW engagement, AI-assisted dev leverage, referral pipeline potential)

## Playbook

1. Document published pricing for: Vyzer (all tiers, especially Family Office), Kubera (Essentials, Black), Monarch Money, Capitally, Asora, Addepar, Eton AtlasFive, Carta Investor Services, Sage Intacct family office. Note request-quote vs. published.
2. Survey custom-build market rates: Upwork/Toptal/Contra senior full-stack hourly rates 2026, agency fixed-bid ranges for custom CRUD admin tools + dashboards, freelancer-disclosed pricing breakdowns, subscription vs. one-time vs. retainer guidance.
3. Synthesize: produce a recommended pricing structure with concrete dollar figures, milestone schedule, customization model, and proposal language.
4. Build the JJ-anchor decision tree: 4 scenarios (free / cheap / no-number / specific-number) with specific dollar recommendations for each.
5. Top of deliverable: 2-3 sentence executive headline with the recommended number Brent can lift directly into the proposal.
6. Cite ≥8 distinct sources across SaaS competitors and custom-build benchmarks. Save citations to `research/sources/pricing_sources.md`.

## Success Criteria

- Executive headline in first 200 words of deliverable
- Concrete dollar recommendations for all 4 JJ-anchor scenarios
- Milestone payment schedule proposed
- Customization upsell model proposed
- ≥8 distinct cited sources
- All quoted competitor numbers tagged published vs. request-quote

## Failure Modes & Recovery

- **Insufficient public pricing data on a specific competitor:** Document as "request-quote, no leaked references found" — do not invent numbers.
- **Conflict between custom-build benchmarks and SaaS competitors:** Surface explicitly, recommend hybrid framing.

## Output Artifacts

- `research/06_pricing_positioning.md`
- `research/sources/pricing_sources.md`
